Caramel Stories Bar and Grill is Garnalwood's latest Sports Bar.Bringing you a fusion of india cusine, mixed with a bar like atmosphere and showing all the

Our Caramel journey begins years aga with a family holiday to India. Whilst there we went to a stall on the acner of a street where a man was serving tea.
People kept calling him. Caramel-wallah; we have all heard the term Chai ___ wallah ( meaning someone who serves tea ) but this was a new one for me.
There was no dress code, no division people of all walks at like were ther, just enjoying this delicious cup of chai.

This is the essence of what we want Caramel to be. Not just your typical bar or a restaurant but a place where everyone can come and enjoy,families and
friends alike.

Since that holoday we have apened several coffee shops under the Caramel Banner over the last 10 and now out of the ashes of this pandemic the world has
